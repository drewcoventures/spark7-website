Pro Tips

Expert strategies from top-ranked players to supercharge your Spark7 score.

🎯

Start from the Edges

Peripheral nodes have fewer connection options — lock them in first before tackling the dense central cluster.

⏱️

Watch the Decay Rate

Charge decays differently in each mode. In Speed Mode, decay is 3x faster — prioritize high-charge nodes first.

🔗

Chain Micro-Sparks

Three small Spark Events in rapid succession count as a Mega Spark and award 5x points.

💡

Save the Surge

Never use a Surge Conduit before round 5. The multiplier is applied to your current score — make it count.

🧩

Play Daily

Daily challenge puzzles award exclusive cosmetics. Completing 7 in a row unlocks the legendary Spark7 border.

🤝

Co-Op Mode

Pair with a friend — one player controls charge injection while the other manages routing. Coordination is everything.
