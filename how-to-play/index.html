How to Play

Earn points by making discs explode.

When the # in a disc matches the quantity of discs in that column OR row, each disc with that same # explodes.

Here, the 4 will explode when we make the stack 4 discs tall. No other discs will explode in this case.

Here, the 3 will explode when we make the row 3 discs wide.

Exploding discs adjacent to a gray stone will make that stone crack, then explode.

When this stack becomes 4 high, both 4s explode, and the adjacent stone at the bottom also explodes, revealing a numbered disc.

When a "buried" disc explodes, others drop to fill its space, often causing a chain reaction ("surge").

For a high score, try to get the largest "surges".

Careful: Every 5 discs, another fresh row of stones will rise from the bottom!

The game is over when any stone or numbered disc protrudes above the top of the grid.
